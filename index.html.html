About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization working towards social welfare and community development. The organization focuses on education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental protection and youth development.

More than 250 interns have been associated with the organization. Through different projects and campaigns, the foundation is helping create a positive impact in society.

Our Projects

1. Seva

Helping people in need by providing food, clothes and other essential items.

2. Bachpanshala

Providing education and care to underprivileged children.

3. Jeev

Supporting and rescuing animals while promoting animal welfare.

4. Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and opportunities.

5. Prakriti

Protecting the environment through plantation drives and clean-up campaigns.

6. Vikas

Helping youth grow through internships and skill-building programs.
